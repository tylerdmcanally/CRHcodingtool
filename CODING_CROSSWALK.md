## App Guardrails Aligned to the Handouts

- MDM level is selected by two of three elements: problems, data, and risk.
- Low data requires either two tests/documents data items or an independent historian.
- Moderate data requires either three category-one data items, independent interpretation, or external discussion.
- High data requires two of three data categories.
- Audio-only encounters require more than 10 minutes of medical discussion documented before the app shows an E/M recommendation.
- Provider time only is counted for time-based selection; nurse or telehealth technician time is not counted.
- Prolonged-service prompts are limited to Medicare/CMS mode; the local telemedicine handouts reviewed here do not define prolonged add-on use for the VA workload telemedicine code families.
